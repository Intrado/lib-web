Welcome to Contact Manager

Contact Manager will let you control what messages you receive and where you receive them. Additionally, any messages which have been sent to you in the last thirty days will be available for review in your Contact Manager account.

Before you begin, make sure you have your ID number and Activation Code so that you can associate your contact information with your new Contact Manager account.

If you do not have an Activation Code and plan to activate your account by phone, you will need to have your ID number and be calling from a phone which is associated with your ID number.

Activating Your Account with an Activation Code

  1. Select the "I have an Activation Code to enter now" option.

  2. Click the "Next" button.

  3. Enter your ID number and Activation Code.

  4. Click the "Add" button. If the information you entered was correct, you will receive a success message.

  5. If you would like to add another contact, click the "Yes" button. If you do not, then click "No".

Activating Your Account by Phone

  1. Select "I do not have an Activation Code and want to activate by phone" option.

  2. Enter the ID number you wish to activate.

  3. Follow the instructions for activating your account by calling the toll-free number listed on the Phone Activation page.

Important:You must call from a phone number which is listed on the page. Also, you must not block your Caller ID. If your Caller ID is blocked, dial *82 before the toll-free number to unblock it.

Checking Your Messages

Any messages that have been sent to you in the last 30 days will appear in the Messages tab. Simply click on the Messages tab, and then click on the action you would like to perform.

Changing Your User Name or Password

  1. Click on the My Account link at the top of the page. You will be taken to the Account Information page which shows your login information.

  2. Click on the Mange Account button near the bottom of the User Information window.

  3. Click the "Change User/Password" link next to Local User.

  4. Enter a new user name to change the name you'll use to log into the system. To change your password, enter a new password and confirm.
