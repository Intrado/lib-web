Enabling the Voice Response Option

Important: Recipient responses are stored on the system for 30 days before being deleted.

Important: You must instruct recipients to press 0 to leave a message. Please view the sample messages below.

The Voice Response option allows recipients to leave a message on the system regarding the notification or survey that they just received. This feature can be enabled on a notification job by clicking the checkbox labeled "Accept Voice Responses", as seen below, when scheduling the job. For surveys, the option can be enabled by clicking the "Accept Voice Responses" checkbox on the Survey Template Builder. Responses are stored on the system for 30 days and can be listened to and downloaded by using the action link next to each job or the Responses tab.

Recording Messages When the Voice Response Option is Enabled

When the Voice Response option is enabled, you must always instruct recipients to press 0 to leave a message as well as what the message should contain. For example:

”r;Hello, this is the attendance office of Anytown High School calling to let you know that your child <<First Name>> <<Last Name>> was marked absent from school today. To leave the reason for this absence, press 0 and leave the following information: your name, your relationship to the child, and the reason for the absence. Thank you. Goodbye.”

If you are sending a survey, the instructions should be included in the Goodbye message. For example:

"Thank you for participating in our survey. If you would like to leave additional comments regarding this survey, please press 0. Be sure to include your name along with your comments. Thank you. Goodbye."

Note: It is recommended that you test your response enabled message on yourself before sending it out in a job to make sure that it sounds correct and your instructions are clear.
