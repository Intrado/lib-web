Building a Survey Template

Important: Only those features that you have been granted access to will display. For access to additional features contact your system administrator.

A Survey Template contains the basic structure of a survey. In the template you can pull together all of your questions as well as any introductory or concluding messages you may have. Before you can begin to assemble your Survey Template, you must have your survey messages and questions recorded. Survey messages and questions are recorded as individual phone messages, just like for any other job. If you haven't recorded your messages and questions yet and need more information about how to do so, please use the Creating a Survey PDF which contains instructions for scripting your survey and recording message pieces. Also, it is highly recommended that you read the Important Tips for Creating Surveys help topic to maximize the success of your survey.

Creating a New Survey Template

To create a new Survey Template after you have recorded all of your messages and questions, click on the Notifications tab followed by the Messages sub tab. At the bottom of the page, you will find the My Survey Templates area as shown above. Click on the Create New Survey Template button found there. You will be taken to the Survey Template Editor to begin configuring your new Survey Template.

The Survey Template Editor

The Survey Template Editor allows you to assemble the basic structure of your survey. Your survey can simultaneously feature both a phone and online component however, for simplicity, in this topic only the Phone portion of the survey will be covered. To learn more about including an online version of your survey, please click here.

Important: Do not create a separate Survey Template for a web version of your survey. This will result in all of your recipients being contacted twice for the same survey.

Settings

  • Name: Enter the name of your Survey Template here.

  • Description: Enter a description of the Survey Template here.

  • Randomize Question Order: Click this checkbox to randomize the order of the questions for each recipient. This can help you gather a more complete picture of how people respond to your questions by more evenly spreading out responses. For example, if you created a non-randomized survey with three questions, but several people hung up after responding to the first two, the third question would have less responses and therefore less data to work with in establishing an average response. By randomizing the survey question order, you can avoid skewed results because early hang ups would not always occur on the same question.

Phone

  • Phone Survey: Check this box if your survey will include a phone component.

  • Leave message on answering machines: Check this if you would like the system to leave the recipient a message if the call is answered by a machine.

  • Play introductory message: Check this and select an introductory message if you have recorded one for this survey. This is the message the recipient will hear before they begin the survey. You may preview the message you selected by clicking the Play button next to the drop down menu.

  • Play good-bye message: Check this and select a message that the recipient will hear after responding to the last question in your survey. You may preview the message you selected by clicking the Play button next to the drop down menu.

  • Allow call recipients to leave a message: When you check this box, recipients will be able to press 0 to leave you a message.

Important: You must instruct recipients to press 0 to leave a message. This should be included in your goodbye message. Also, you must include a goodbye message in your survey template in order to enable this feature.

Important: Recipient responses are stored on the system for 30 days before being deleted.

Web

  • Web Survey: Click this if your template will include a web version. Web surveys options are covered in detail in the More on Web Survey Template Features help topic.

Questions

  • Report Label: In this field, enter the name for the question as you would like it to appear in your job report. You may find it easier to use labels indicative of the question's topic.

  • Phone: Select the phone message that contains the question from the drop down menu. You may click the Play button to preview your question and make sure it's the right one.

  • Response: Select the response possibilities for your question from the drop down menu. For example, your question is, "From the following colors, please select which is your favorite. Press 1 for red. Press 2 for yellow. Press 3 for green. Press 4 for blue." You would select 1-4 in the Response drop down.

  • Actions: Click the Add button to add another question to your survey. Click Delete to remove a question from your survey.

When you have completed your template are ready to send out your survey, click the Schedule Survey button at the bottom or top of your screen. If you would like to save your template to be sent at another time, click the Save for Later button.
