Scheduling a Survey Job

Important: Only those features that you have been granted access to will display. For access to additional features contact your system administrator.

Once you have completed building your Survey Template, you are ready to send your survey job.

You may access the Survey Scheduler by clicking the Schedule Survey button on your Survey Template or clicking the Notifications tab followed by the Surveys sub tab. Then just enter the appropriate data into the form. Click the Proceed to Confirmation button to confirm your settings and send your job or click the Save for Later button to save your job for another time.

Note: It's a good idea to test your survey on yourself before you send it to your list of recipients.

Settings:

  • Survey Name: Enter a name for your Survey Job here.

  • Description: Enter an optional description for your survey here.

  • Job Type: Choose the type of job that you are sending.

  • Survey Template: Select the Survey Template you wish to use for this job.

  • List: Select the List of contacts you wish to receive your survey.

  • Start Date: Enter the date you would like your Survey Job to being running on.

  • Number of days to run: Select the number of days you would like your job to run. Make sure you allow enough time for the system to contact all of your recipients.

  • Survey Time Window: Select the earliest and latest times you would like the system to contact recipients with your survey.

  • Email a report after when the survey completes: Checking this box will have the system email a report to the email address associated with your account when the job has finished running.

Phone:

  • Maximum attempts: Choose the number of times you would like the system to try to call a recipient before giving up.

  • Caller ID: Enter the phone number as you would like it to appear in the recipient's caller ID.

When you are finished entering the information into the Survey Scheduler fields, click the Proceed to Confirmation button at the top or bottom of the screen to review and confirm your selections and send the job. You will see a screen like the one shown below. If you decide you would like to save your job to send at a later time, simply click the Save for Later button.
