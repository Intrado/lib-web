Step3 - Send Yourself an Email

Now that you've made a list and called yourself, it's time to try sending yourself an email. You should have included your email address when you entered your information while building a list. If you didn't include your email address, don't worry. You can go back to your list and edit your contact information to enter an email address. Just click the Preview button followed by the pencil icon next to your name to edit your information.

Send Yourself an Email

  1. Click the Notifications tab followed by the Jobs subtab.

  2. Click the Create New Job button in the My Jobs section.

  3. Click the email icon in the Job Editor.

  4. Enter a name for your job and choose a job type.

  5. Select the list you made for contacting yourself.

  6. Click the Create a Message radio button.

  7. Enter an address for reply, subject, and body for your email, as shown below.
