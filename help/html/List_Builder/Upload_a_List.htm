Subsections in H4

This guide contains useful tips to help you use the system to effectively communicate with your contacts.

Subsubsection headers

  • Guide Help: If you are unsure about any process, you can click the Guide button in the upper right corner of the screen, opening the inline help which can guide you with useful steps and information. The guide will follow you as you make your way through a screen, giving you the information you need when you need it.

  • Label Help:  For most fields and options, you can mouse over the field name for more information about what the field should contain and what it is used for.

  • Form Confirmation: Clear confirmation on every field lets you know that you've correctly filled in the field and helps you know if you've made any mistakes.
