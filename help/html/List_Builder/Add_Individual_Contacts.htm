Add Individual Contacts

Important: Only those features that you have been granted access to will display. For access to additional features contact your system administrator.

This topic covers how to add individuals to a list who are not covered by simple rules-based lists. This includes:

  • Manually entering contacts in the List Editor.

  • Using your Address Book.

  • Adding individuals by searching your contact database.

To use this tutorial, you should first follow the steps in Create a List. If you have done this, you will be in the List Editor, shown below,  where you will find the tools you'll need.

Enter Contacts

The Enter Contacts tool can be used to manually add individuals and their contact information to your lists.

  • Click the Enter Contacts button in the Additional Tools section of the List Editor.

  • Enter the individual's contact information into the appropriate fields. You may also choose how this person is contacted based on notification type preferences by checking the appropriate boxes.

  • To add this person to your personal Address Book, make sure Save to Address Book is checked. You can add this individual to future lists by simply using your Address Book.

  • Click the Done button at the bottom of the screen to add this person to your list.

  • To add another person to your list, repeat the process.

Note: To remove a manually added individual from your list, just uncheck the box next to the individual's name in the Additions section of the List Editor. Refreshing the screen will show the person is no longer in your list.

 

Adding Contacts from your Address Book

The Open Address Book button opens your address book. Your account has a personal address book where you can store contact information for individuals you want to include in notifications, but who are not included in your simple lists.

  • Click the Open Address Book button located in the Additional List Tools section.

  • After selecting the contacts you wish to add, finish by clicking the Done button.

Note: Your Address Book is also available by clicking the Address Book link in the upper right corner of the screen. You can add individuals to your Address Book at any time by clicking the Add New Contact button at the top of the Contacts window.

Adding Individuals by Searching

Quick Pick

Quick Pick allows you to rapidly search for individuals in your database by entering their name, phone number, ID number, or email address.

  • Click the Quick Pick button located in the Additional List Tools section to access the List Search screen

  • Enter the individual’s name, phone number, ID number, or email address in the search field.

  • Click the Search button to display the results of your search.

  • Select the individual for your list by checking the checkbox next to their name.

  • Click Done to save your changes.

Search by Rules

You can use the Search by Rules option to filter through your contacts and find individuals you want to add to your list. This can be useful if you need to find an individual when you don’t have all the information you need to just use QuickPick. For example, if you need to find someone where you only know their last name or you aren’t sure quite how to spell their last name, you can search with a rule which includes only their last name or the first letter(s) of their last name.

  • Click the Search by Rules button located in the Additional List Tools section. This will redirect you to the List Search window.

  • To filter your contacts by rules, select the search field and the comparison operator you wish to use (i.e. "is not", etc.) in the Criteria area. Then select the data items that you wish to include in your search rule, and click the Add button.

  • When you locate the person (or people) who you want to include in your list, simply click the checkbox to the left of their name. Repeat until you have selected everyone that you want included on your list.

  • When you are finished, click the Done button at the bottom of the window, and you will be returned to the List Editor. From there you can save your list or continue by adding rules or more individuals to your list.
