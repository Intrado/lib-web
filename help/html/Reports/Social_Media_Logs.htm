Social Media Logs

The Social Media Log shows you exactly what was posted to which destination and by which user for any given job or date. You may also download a CSV of the report.

The Social Media Log is available under the Reports tab by clicking on the Social Media Log link.

If you would like to view the full content of the Facebook or Twitter post, simply click on the Facebook Content or Twitter Content text in the report. The full content of the posts is also included in the CSV report you may download.
