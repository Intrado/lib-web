SMS Reports

Important: Only those features that you have been granted access to will display. For access to additional features contact your system administrator.

You may access a simple report for SMS Jobs in just a few steps.

  1. Click the Reports tab.

  2. Click the SMS Report subtab.

  3. Select the job you would like to view.

It is important to note that it is not possible to view whether or not the recipient actually read the message. The system can only tell that the SMS message was sent to the recipient's phone number. The SMS Report, as shown below, shows the message that was sent and the list of recipients to whom it was sent.
