Start Page

The Start Page, shown below, is divided into three basic sections.

EasyStart & Emergency

  • The large EasyStart button in the upper left corner is for sending any non-emergency jobs. It will take you into the MessageSender which will guide you through sending your job.

  • The Emergency button is for sending Emergency jobs only.

Important: Using the Emergency button will change the introductory message for your job, stating that it is an emergency, and contact recipients by the methods chosen specifically for emergency messages. It is best practice to only use Emergency in the event of an emergency.

Jobs Timeline

The timeline at the top of the page graphically shows your job history and can provide a quick look at what's currently running.

Recent Activity

The Recent Activity section shows all recent activity on your account, messages from the system, and more. Clicking on anything in the list will take you to that item in the system. For example, clicking on a job will take you to the report for that job or clicking on a saved list will open up the list. You may filter what is displayed by clicking on the Filter By options on the left side of the window.
