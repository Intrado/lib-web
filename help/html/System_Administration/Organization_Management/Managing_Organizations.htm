Managing Organizations

Larger organizations, such as school districts or universities, typically have smaller internal groups into which users and contacts may be organized. The organization manager allows you to manage these groups and see what they are linked to.

You may access the Organization Manager by clicking on the Admin tab followed by the Settings subtab. The Organization Manager is in the System column.

The system uses organizations to:

  • Organize Users - Users can be associated to particular organizations. You can limit what data a user can see based on the organization they are associated with.

  • Making lists - Organizations can be used as a filter when making lists. For example, you may want to make a list to call all of the students in a particular school or all of the staff in a particular department.

  • Publish and Subscribe - The publish and subscribe feature works on an organizational level. Users may publish items for other users in their organization.

Organization Manager options:

  • Rename - Allows you to change the name of an organization

  • Merge - Merge an organization into another organization. Note: Merge cannot be undone.

  • Delete - Deletes an organization. You may not delete an organization that has an association.

  • Associations - Click this to view where in the system the organization is being used.
