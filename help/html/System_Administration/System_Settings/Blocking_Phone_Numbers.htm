Blocking Phone Numbers

Important: Blocking a phone number will result in this phone number not being contacted for any reason by any user. It is a systemwide block.

Blocking certain phone numbers from being called can be easily done from the Blocked Numbers List found under the System tab. After clicking the Blocked Lists subtab, click the Phone Calls / Text Messages Link, shown below.

Clicking the Phone Calls / Text Messages link will take you to the Blocked List for phone numbers.

On the Blocked Numbers List page you can view a list of all of the numbers which have been blocked by any user and the reason given for blocking the number. Adding or removing numbers from the list is a very simple process.

How to Block a Phone Number

  1. Enter the phone number, including the area code, in the Number field.

  2. Enter a brief reason for blocking the number.

  3. Click the Add button.

How to Unblock a Phone Number

  1. Find the number you wish to unblock in the list.

  2. Click the Delete link in the Actions area to the right of the number.
