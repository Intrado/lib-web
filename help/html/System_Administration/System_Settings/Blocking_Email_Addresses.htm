Blocking Email Addresses

Important: Blocking an email address will result in this email address not being contacted for any reason by any user. It is a systemwide block.

Blocking certain email addresses from receiving messages can be easily done from the Blocked Numbers List found under the System tab.

On the Blocked List page you can view a list of all of the email addresses which have been blocked by any user and the reason given for blocking the address. Adding or removing addresses from the list is a very simple process.

How to Block an Email Address

  1. Enter the email address in the Email field.

  2. Enter a brief reason for blocking the email.

  3. Click the Add button.

How to Unblock an Email Address

  1. Find the email address you wish to unblock in the list.

  2. Click the Delete link in the Actions area to the right of the entry.
