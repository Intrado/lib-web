Review and Maintenance of Blocked Lists

Regular review of the blocked lists will help ensure that your contact data is accurate and up to date.

Can I export the blocked lists to view as a spreadsheet?

The blocked list allows you to identify the person associated with the blocked data, including their school association. You can export this data as a CSV file which can be opened with your preferred spreadsheet software, such as Microsoft Excel.

  1. From the phone or email blocked list screen, click the Display Contacts option in the list area towards the bottom of the screen. This will display the details of the contacts in the system who are associated with each entry in the blocked list.

  2. Click the CSV Download link.

How do I determine who is associated with each entry in the blocked list?

The Display Contacts checkbox lets you see the contact details for each person associated with a blocked number or email address.

If a blocked number or email address does not display a school and/or student ID, then this entry is either no longer associated with a student in your system or was entered into the system via a user's address book or manual CSV file upload. It is recommended that you leave these unassociated records in your blocked lists.

Can I delete phone numbers or email addresses from the blocked list?

If a phone number or email address is no longer associated with any contacts in your account, it is safe to delete the record from the blocked list. You can determine whether this is the case by clicking the Display Contacts checkbox. You may safely delete any records that do not display data in both the student ID and name fields and which do not display the magnifying glass icon which allows you to view contact details.

You should always attempt to correct data at the source (such as your SIS or HR system) whenever practical. Your regularly scheduled data imports will then automatically update the incorrect data so that you do not need to manually add or delete information in the blocked lists. Even then, there may be times when a parent wishes to have a certain phone number or email address blocked from all notifications and it is not practical to remove it from the system (eg. a work phone number). In these situations, you can simply add the information to the blocked list.
