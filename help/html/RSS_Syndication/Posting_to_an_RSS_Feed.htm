Posting to an RSS Feed

The RSS messaging component allows you to syndicate your messages in feeds which people may subscribe to. Publishing messages to an RSS feed is easy. You can create feed content while creating your other message components in MessageSender or while creating reusable messages in Message Builder.

Creating RSS Feed Content

The Quick and Simple Way

Creating RSS Feed content can be as simple as any other step in MessageSender. Just click the blue EasyStart button, choose how you'd like to create your messages, just as before. Then prior to sending your job, you'll simply need to choose a category for your feed content.

Advanced Posting

You can create an RSS Feed component while creating other message content using Message Builder.

  1. Navigate to the Message Builder by clicking the Notifications tab followed by the Messages subtab.

  2. Click "Create a Message" to create a new Message Group.

  3. Enter a name for your Message Group and click "Next".

  4. You will be taken to the Message Editor where you will see a grid of Message Content types. The green plus icon next to each of the column headings will take you to a wizard for creating content of that type. Click the "Guide" button from within any of the wizards for instructions for creating content for that content type. This help topic will focus solely on posting to an RSS Feed.

  5. Click the green plus icon next to Feed. Just like posting to a social media site, you may attach Page Media to your RSS Feed post. If you know you will be creating Page message content, you may want to discuss what will be found at the link attached to your Feed content. Pages allow you to create a web page with a lot more information than will fit in simple RSS Feed content, including audio and file downloads.

  6. Click "Done" once you've finished creating your RSS feed content. It will be saved to your message group to be used any time you create a job using a saved message.
