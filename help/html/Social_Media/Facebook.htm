Facebook

Important: This feature is only visible if your system includes the Social Media feature. Social Media is a new feature from SchoolMessenger that allows you to leverage the power of social media to deliver your message. For more information regarding the Social Media feature, please call 1-800-920-3897.

Facebook is a great way to reach out to your contacts who are avid Facebook users. This feature allows you to connect to your Facebook account and post to your Wall or to any Facebook Pages where you are an administrator. Contacts who are your Facebook friends or who have liked your Facebook Page can then see your message when they log in.

Posting to Facebook

The Quick and Simple Post

Posting to Facebook can be as simple as any other step in MessageSender. Just click the blue EasyStart button, choose how you'd like to create your messages, just as before. Then prior to sending your job, you'll find new steps for Facebook and Twitter. Entering text for posting on these sites is exactly like writing your own SMS message.

Advanced Posting

Alternatively, if you would like to use the Message Editor to create your social media posts, you may use more advanced features, such as the Page message type with embedded Page Media.

  1. Navigate to the Message Builder by clicking the Notifications tab followed by the Messages subtab.

  2. Click "Create a Message" to create a new Message Group.

  3. Enter a name for your Message Group and click "Next".

  4. You will be taken to the Message Editor where you will see a grid of Message Content types. The green plus icon next to each of the column headings will take you to a wizard for creating content of that type. Click the "Guide" button from within any of the wizards for instructions for creating content for that content type. This help topic will focus solely on posting to Facebook.

  5. Click the green plus icon next to Facebook to create a new Facebook post. If you know you will be creating Page message content, you may want to discuss what will be found at the link attached to your Facebook post. Pages allow you to create a web page with a lot more information than will fit in a simple Facebook post, including audio and file downloads..

  6. Click "Done" once you've finished your Facebook post. It will be saved to your message group to be used any time you create a job using a saved message.
