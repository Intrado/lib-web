Sending Push Notifications

Important: Only those features that you have been granted access to will display. Additionally, features in this section of the help manual refer to integration with another product.

What is a Push Notification?

Push notifications are messages that will appear as notifications on devices with your custom mobile app. They're sent to specific categories so that only users who have subscribed to the category will receive the notification. For example, if your app has a set of categories corresponding to specific schools, users who have subscribed to content for a specific school will also receive push notifications for that school.   

To send push notifications, each category from your app must be mapped to a related RSS Feed. Then whenever you post to the RSS Feed, your post will also be sent as a push notification to all of the app categories linked to the feed.

How to Send a Push Notification

Creating push notifications is no different from creating any other type of message.

  • While creating a Broadcast from the desktop, click the Social button during the message creation step. This will take you to the screen shown below.

  • Select the Feed/Desktop/Push option.

  • Enter your message content and select the RSS feed categories you wish to post to.

  • Click the Save Social Message button when you're done.
