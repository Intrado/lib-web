About Custom Mobile Apps

Important: Only those features that you have been granted access to will display. Additionally, features in this section of the help manual refer to integration with another product.

What are custom mobile apps?

Custom mobile apps are another product which we offer to help you communicate with members of your community. We work with you to create and deploy fully customized apps for Android and iOS devices which can be downloaded for free from the Google Play and Apple iTunes app stores. If you do not have a custom mobile app and  would like more information about custom mobile apps, please contact your sales representative.

If you do have a custom mobile app, this section of the manual is geared towards helping you take advantage of the features that let you send new content to your app's users.
