Welcome!

This guide contains useful tips to help you use the system to effectively communicate with your contacts.

Help

User friendly help is one way we try to ensure your experience is the easiest and best it can be. You will find that throughout the system, help is always nearby.

  • Guide Help: If you are unsure about any process, you can click the Guide button in the upper right corner of most screens, opening the in line help which can guide you with useful steps and information. The guide will follow you as you make your way through a screen, giving you the information you need when you need it.

  • Label Help:  For most fields and options, you can mouse over the field name for more information about what the field should contain and what it is used for.

  • Form Confirmation:  Clear confirmation on every field lets you know that you've correctly filled in the field and helps you know if you've made any mistakes.

Printable Step-by-Step Training Guides

  • Printable Training PDF Guides: Printable PDF guides can be found in the "Printable PDFs" section of this manual. Please go there for easy to use step-by-step guides that will get you sending messages and even using advanced features in no time!
