Create a List

Important: Some menus or features shown may not appear in your account, as only those to which you have been granted access will be visible.  For access to additional features, please contact your account administrator.

Important: Lists are created from the data imported to your account through the Data Import Manager found in the "Admin" tab under "Imports".  If your account has no import files containing contact info, please contact your account administrator or support.

Note: If you are new to the SchoolMessenger system and trying to send a simple message, you might prefer the Quickly Send a Basic Message topic which covers creating a new Broadcast from the Dashboard.

 

Getting Started

Creating a list begins with the "List Builder" utility, found in the "Lists" subsection of the "Broadcasts" tab.

 

Add New List

Begin by clicking the "Add New List" button in the upper right of the My Lists section.

 

List Editor

    1. The first step to creating a list is to give it a good, meaningful name that will help you and other users easily and accurately identify its recipients.  Names like "All Students & Guardians - General", "Elementary Guardians Only" or "High School Band" will make it easy to identify later.

    2. The "Description" can be used to add further detail to assist in identifying the specific recipients.

 

There are several options for how you can add contacts to a list.

    • Use "Rules" to select your contacts.

    • Enter Contacts by typing them.

    • Choose contacts from you personal SchoolMessenger address book.

    • Search for contacts in a shared system contact database using rules.

    • Upload a list of contacts using a CSV file.

 

List Content - Use Rules to Select Your Contact Records

    1. Click on the "Field" menu to drop down the list of all the fields available within your contact data.

 

    1. Choose the field value which is going to most help you filter down to just the individuals for whom you want this list to broadcast.  In our example, we're building a "High School Band" list, so we'll start by selecting "School" from the list so we can choose just the High School.

    2. After checking all selections that apply, click the "Add" button.

If all you need is that one rule to accurately select the contacts you need, you can move on to the Target Recipients instructions.

Following our example, we need to add one more rule to make sure we filter down to just the Band students in the High School.

    1. Use the "Field" menu to select the value like in step 2.  We select "Activities" for our example.

    2. Select the appropriate "Criteria":  "equals" to get an exact match, "does not equal" to get all records except those with a specific value... or use "contains" when a field could contain more than one entry, like in our example: a student could certainly be in more than one extracurricular activity.

    3. Type what you are looking for into the "Value" field.  Whatever you type must exactly match or no results will be produced. (Value is not case-sensitive)

    4. Once all your rule settings are correct, click "Add".

Note: Once you have selected a rule for a field, you will no longer be able to add more rules that include that field.  You may, however, create multiple lists. ie: To send a broadcast to the Varsity Football and Jr. Varsity Football team, you could create a separate list for both.

Once you have finished adding rules, you can check the "Total" under the List Name and Description above the List Contents.  This will show you how many contacts are selected under the current rules.

Clicking the "Preview" button will show you all the contact records based on your list settings.

You can view and edit individual contact records within the list preview.

 

Target Recipients

For Schools/Districts which are taking advantage of the SchoolMessenger Guardian Data Model, lists can be created which target just contacts/students, just guardians, or both.

If you select "Contacts", then only the contacts/students will receive messages broadcast to this list.  If you select "Associated Guardians", only the Guardians will get the messages.

The "Guardian Category Restriction" allows you great flexibility to create a list that targets only specific Guardian types.  For example: a message which is just general information can be targeted for just the primary guardians, a more urgent notification can target both primary and secondary guardians, and for emergency notifications, all boxes could be checked (or all left unchecked) to ensure that all of the guardians are contacted.

Important:  If your school/district does not use the "Guardian Data Model", you should just ignore the options under "Target Recipients".  Simply leave Target Recipients set to "Both" and Guardian Category Restrictions unchecked - this will ensure that all your recipients points of contact are included in broadcasts to this list.

Once your list Total and recipients match your requirement, click the "Done" button at the bottom of the screen.  You will be returned to the main List Builder screen and your new list will appear in the My Lists section.  This list can now be selected with just a single click when sending a broadcast.

 

Additional List Tools

Use these tools, located at the bottom of the "List Editor" to add contacts who may not be accessible by simple rules or who are not part of your SchoolMessenger import files.

  • Enter Contacts: This tool lets you enter individual contacts into the list and save them to your Address Book for the future.

  • Open Address Book: This lets you add individuals whose information you've saved in your Address Book.

  • Quick Pick: Use this to search for specific individuals in the system when you know their name, ID number, email address, or phone number.

  • Search by Rules: If you need to add an individual, but you don't have specific information, you can use rules to narrow your search. For example, if you need to add an individual by name, but aren't quite sure how to spell it beyond the first few letters, searching by rules can help.

  • Upload List: This tool lets you upload a CSV file of contacts.

 

Enter Contacts

Clicking on "Enter Contacts" will open the "Address Book Editor" to allow you to manually type in all the contact information for any person you wish to include in your list. This is covered in more detail in the help topic for Address Book.

 

Open Address Book

Clicking on "Open Address Book" will open "My Address Book" to allow you to select any persons in your Address Book Contacts that you wish to include in your list. Simply click into the box in front of any contact to select them for inclusion in the list you're creating.  More detail can be found in the help topic for Address Book.

Once you've finished selecting the Address Book contacts you need, click on the "Done" button at the top or bottom of the Contacts section.

 

Quick Pick & Search by Rules

Clicking on "Quick Pick" or "Search by Rules" will open the "List Search" options screen.  From there you can "Search by Rules" (using the exact same steps illustrated above), "Search for Person" by typing in a contacts first or last name, or "Show All Contacts" to add contacts from a list of all contacts loaded into your account by placing a check in front of their record.

  1. Sort By: Use the three drop-down menus to create a sorting priority to help you locate your desired contacts.  Combine them to further narrow down your search.

  2. Show/Hide Fields: Used to add or remove other fields within the grid view on the screen to even further help you narrow down to your desired contacts.

  3. Pages: Remember that only 100 records can be displayed on this screen at one time - you may need to change to another page to continue searching for your desired contacts.

Important:  The "Search Results" feature includes all Contacts/Students and Staff who have been imported into your account.

Note: When searching via the "Show All Contacts" option, any contacts you have already selected by "Rules" will automatically appear with a check-mark in front of their record.

To include a contact in the list you are creating, simply click to check the box at the front of that contacts row.  After you have finished checking off all the contacts you need, you must click the "Done" button above the results in the "Search Options" section.  You will then be returned to the List Editor and your total contact count should reflect any additions.

 

Upload List

Clicking on "Upload List" will open the Upload List screen where you can load a CSV data file from your computer which contains the contacts to whom you wish to broadcast.  This process is detailed in the Upload List page of this guide.

 

After Saving Your List

Once you have finished selecting all the contacts needed to create your list, and have targeted the appropriate recipients, click on the "Done" button at the bottom of the List Editor.  You will be returned to the List Builder screen and will see your list has now been saved in "My Lists".

Any lists appearing in the My Lists section will be available to instantly select when setting up a Broadcast.  You will also find the options to "Edit", "Preview", "Publish" or "Delete" lists.

Note: For lists to which you have subscribed, the only options that will be available are "Preview" and "Un-Subscribe".  You cannot make edits or delete lists which were created by other users.
