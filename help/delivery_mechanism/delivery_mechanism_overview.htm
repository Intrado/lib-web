Delivery Mechanism Overview

The Delivery Mechanism is a module that executes a delivery task. Your system contains more than one Delivery Mechanism to complete its various tasks. Each Delivery Mechanism has its own pool of delivery resources available to it. For example, a phone delivery mechanism will have a pool of available phone lines that it may work with to deliver your phone message to the specified recipient. Delivery Mechanisms are the machines that run in the background executing the jobs you specify through the SchoolMessenger CommSuite.

The Delivery Mechanism Configuration Screen is not accessible from the regular CommSuite interface. Its location is unique to each system. Your SchoolMessenger representative can give you the access information for this screen. You will generally not need to access this screen as it is used primarily for configuring your system by your SchoolMessenger representative.

Important: Your Delivery Mechanism settings were configured by your SchoolMessenger representative and are designed to be completely maintenance free. Editing these settings could cause your system to not work properly.
