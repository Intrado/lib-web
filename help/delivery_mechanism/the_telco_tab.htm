The Telco Tab

Important: The settings you see have been established by your SchoolMessenger representative and are designed to be maintenance free. Editing these settings could cause your system to not function properly.

Clicking the Telco tab will take you to the Settings sub-tab by default. The Settings sub-tab has many sections:

  • TTS ports – This corresponds to the number of port licenses you purchased with your CommSuite system. Setting this to a number greater than the number or actual ports you have licensed will cause this to stop working.

  • Local Area Code – Your local area code.

  • Local Outside Line – If you must dial a digit for an outside line enter that digit here. For example, if you must dial 9 to get an outside line with your PBX you would enter a 9 here.

  • Local Strip Area Code – Check to remove the first 3 digits (area code) from the numbers you are calling.

  • Long Distance Outside Line – Enter your long distance outside line here

  • Long Distance Add One – Check to dial one before dialing a long distance number.

In addition to the Settings sub-tab, there is the Routes sub-tab. Here you can configure special routes for just about any prefix and also block a phone number by stripping all of its digits. You can strip up to 99 characters. You must include the area code in the match to correctly block certain numbers.
