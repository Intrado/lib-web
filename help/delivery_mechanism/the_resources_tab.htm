The Resources Tab

Important: The settings you see have been established by your SchoolMessenger representative and are designed to be maintenance free. Editing these settings could cause your system to not function properly.

The Resources tab is where you can see, disable, and enable which resources are available to the Delivery Mechanism you are logged into. For example, a phone Delivery Mechanism would show a list of available phone lines.

When you click on the Resources tab you will see the Status sub-tab by default. On this page you can view all of the available resources. There is a link to the right of each resource to disable or enable that resource. These settings are managed by your SchoolMessenger representative.

In addition to the Status sub-tab, there is a Create sub-tab. This area is used by your SchoolMessenger representative and should not be edited.
