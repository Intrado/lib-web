Notification Summaries

A Notification Summary is a report that shows you the results of any active or completed job in the system. The quickest way to view a Notification Summary is to click on the Report link next to the job for which you would like a report. You may also access the Notification Summary for any job, including archived jobs, by clicking on the Reports Tab.

Accessing the Notification Summary for and Active, Pending, or Completed Job

Simply click the Report link next to the job as shown above.

Accessing the Notification Summary for an Archived Job

  1. Click on the Reports tab. You will be taken to the Report Builder as shown below.

  1. Click on the Notification Summary link to access the Notification Summary screen as shown below.

  1. Check the box labeled "Show Archived Jobs"

  2. Find the archived job in the list. You may search by Job name or the date it was sent. Additionally, you may use the filter to select only jobs of a specific type.

Understanding the Notification Summary

The Notification Summary, shown below, is divided into four sections designed to give you a quick overview of what happened with your job. You may also find additional information by using the Related Links.

Related Links

The Related Links section contains links which are also relevant to your report.

PDF: This link will allow you to download a PDF version of the report. You will need a PDF reader, such as Adobe Acrobat, to view this report.

Time Distribution: This link opens a pop up window that displays the report results in relation to the times that the calls were sent. An example is shown below.

Not Contacted: This link shows a report of recipients that the system was not able to contact.

Filter By

If you used any filtering to access this report, it will be displayed in this area.

Summary

The Summary section shows a brief overview of the job.

Job Name: The name of the job displays here.

Type: The type of job, such as Attendance or Emergency, displays here.

Submitted by: This area shows the name of the user who submitted the job.

Start Date: This is the date and time that the job started running.

End Date: This is the date and time that the job was completed.

Status: This tells whether the job is running currently or completed.

Ind's on List: This is the number of individuals on the list or recipients.

# of Phones: This is the number of phone numbers that were to be contacted with this job.

# of Emails: This is the number of email addresses that were to be contacted with this job.

Breakdown of Results

This section contains a breakdown of the total calls sent by the job in text and graphic form.

Answered: The recipient answered the phone and listened to the message.

Machine: The recipient's answering machine received the message.

Busy: The recipient's phone was busy.

No Answer: Nobody answered the phone.

Disconnect: This is typically a number which has been disconnected.

Unknown: Unknown most commonly results from the system encountering one of the following circumstances:

The system attempted to deliver a message to a full voice mail.

The number dialed was answered by an automated attendant message that was requesting some sort of interaction.

The call was answered by an answering machine with a greeting message which was longer than 45 seconds.

The recipient hung up before the message started playing.

The local phone company switch rejected the call.

The local phone company switch was unable to connect the call within 45 seconds.

The number is disconnected, but the local phone company doesn't return standard digital disconnect indicator codes.

Duplicate: The number of recipients with duplicate phone numbers, such as siblings in different grades. Unchecking the "Skip duplicate phone numbers" box when scheduling your job will tell the system to not filter out the duplicate numbers.

Blocked: The number of blocked phone numbers that were included in this job. Blocked phone numbers are numbers that the system may not call because they have been blocked in the System tab.

No Phone #: The number of recipients in your list who do not have phone numbers in the database.

No Phone Selected: This individual did not have any data in the phone fields that were selected for inclusion in this job. Use the Job Type Manager to determine which phone numbers are selected for each type of notification job.

Not Attempted: These numbers have not been called either because the system hasn't processed them yet, the job's scheduled time ran out before they could be processed, or the job was cancelled.
