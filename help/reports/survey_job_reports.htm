Survey Job Reports

A Survey Job report is a special type of Standard Job Report that additionally graphically displays the results of a survey. It can be viewed by either clicking the Report link next to any active or completed Survey Job, or clicking the Reports tab followed by the Job Report sub tab, then selecting the name of the survey job from the drop down menu at the top of the page.

Report Summary

The Report Summary section graphically displays what your job is currently doing and how close it is to completing. If your job does not contain any email messages, then the email portion will not display. Conversely, if you are only sending email, the phone graphs will not display.

People to Contact

Contacted: Recipients who are shown as Contacted include those listed as having answered the phone and those where the system left an answering machine message.

Not Contacted: These are the recipients who have not yet received a call as well as those with busy signals, disconnected numbers, or other problems preventing them from being contacted.

Duplicates: These are recipients who share the same phone numbers as another. For example, two siblings in the same school might have identical contact information. This value is only displayed if the Skip Duplicates checkbox was selected when the job was scheduled.

Numbers to Call

Completed: This means that the system has successfully contacted the recipient or left a message.

Remaining: These are numbers remaining to be called.

Emails to Send

Completed: Email messages have been successfully sent to these recipients.

Remaining: These recipients are remaining to be processed.

Survey Results

The Survey Results section graphically displays the results of your survey.

Participation

This graph shows the number of people who participated in your survey.

Responses

This section has a graph for each question with the responses given. The responses are labeled with the number the participant pressed and the percentage of participants who selected the response. To display this information in a table layout without graphs, as shown at the beginning of this help topic, click the Show Table link.

Report Detail

This section displays a more detailed version of the information from the Report Summary section. As in the Report Summary, if your job does not contain any email messages, then the email portion will not display. Conversely, if you are only sending email, the phone graphs will not display. The image below shows a sample phone graph.

Phone:

Phone #s: The total number of phone numbers in the list associated with this job.

Answered: The recipient answered the phone and listened to the message.

Machine: The recipient's answering machine received the message.

No Answer: Nobody answered the phone.

Busy: The recipient's phone was busy.

Disconnect: This is typically a number which has been disconnected.

Failed: This indicates a temporary telco error and is usually resolved on the next attempt.

Not Attempted: These numbers have not been called either because the system hasn't processed them yet, the job's scheduled time ran out before they could be processed, or the job was cancelled.

Email:

People: The total number of recipients in the list associated with this job.

Email Addresses: The total number of email addresses that will receive email messages.

% Sent: The percentage of email messages currently sent.

Contact Log:

View: Displays a detailed call log for this job. More information can be found in the Understanding Call Logs help topic.

Download CSV file: Download a CSV (comma separated value) file of the call log that includes the individual responses to each question. This is useful for performing your own analyses in spreadsheet programs such as Excel.
