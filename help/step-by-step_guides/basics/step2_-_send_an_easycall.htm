Step2 - Send an EasyCall

Now that you've made a list for calling yourself, you're ready to try sending an EasyCall. Before you use EasyCall, make sure you're at a phone with a direct dial phone number (no extensions to dial).

Send Yourself an EasyCall

  1. Click the Start Tab to go to your Start Page.

  2. Click the EasyCall button in the QuickStart section. The following window will pop up.

  1. Enter a name for your job and select a job type.

  2. Select your list.

  3. Enter the direct access phone number where you are located.

  4. Click the Call Me to Record button.

  5. When the system calls you, just follow the prompts to complete your recording. After you finish and hang up, you will see the following screen where you can review and confirm your EasyCall job.

  1. If everything looks correct, click the Submit button.

  2. You may view the status of your job on the Notification Jobs page by clicking on the Notifications tab followed by the Jobs tab.

  3. Congratulations! You should receive a call at the phone number in your list shortly. You're ready to proceed to Step 3 - Send Yourself an Email.
