Introduction to Basic Training

In basic training, you will learn how to make a list to contact yourself. Then you will call your list and send an email. This is the best way to familiarize yourself with the workings of the system and will help you quickly prepare to send actual messages.

Once you've completed basic training, you will be ready to learn how to use popular features, such as making a list using rules to call groups of people in the database.

Now, let's get started!
