Editing an Existing Job

In the Notification Jobs screen, any jobs that are currently saved in the system can be edited, deleted, or cancelled.

Editing a Job:

Clicking the edit link in the Actions section to the right of the job will take you to the appropriate Job Editor screen.

Deleting a Job:

You may delete a repeating job by clicking the Delete link in the Actions section to the right of the job.

Canceling a Job:

Click the Cancel link in the Actions section to the right of the job to cancel it. In the event of an error setting up your job, you can cancel a running job by clicking this link.

Archiving a Job:

You may archive a completed job by clicking on the Archive link in the Actions section. This will cause the job to no longer appear on the Notification Jobs page, but you will still be able to see its data by creating a Notification Report.
