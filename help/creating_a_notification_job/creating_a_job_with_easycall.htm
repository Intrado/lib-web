Creating a Job with EasyCall

Note: In order to use EasyCall, you must have a list already saved in the system.

Important: Only those features that you have been granted access to will display. For access to additional features contact your system administrator.

The EasyCall feature can be found on your Start Page. To create a new EasyCall job, just click the EasyCall button.

After you click the Easy Call button the EasyCall window will pop up.

Using EasyCall:

  1. Enter a name for your job.

  2. Select the job type.

  3. Select your list of recipients. If your list has not been created yet, you will need to create a new list.

  4. In the Call Me At field, enter the direct access phone number where you are located.

  5. Check the Record in Additional Languages checkbox if you would like to create a multilingual job. Select the additional language you would like to record and click the Add button to the right of the drop down menu. If you select the wrong language, just click the Delete button next to the language.

  6. Click the Call Me to Record button. While the system is calling you, you will see the following screen.

  1. When the system calls you, follow the prompts to complete your recordings. After you finish and hang up, you will be taken to the Review and Confirm Selections screen.

  1. Here you may confirm that your message sounds correct and submit your job by clicking the Submit Job button. If you need to change a setting in your job, click the Modify Job Settings button. If you would like to wait and send your job later, click the Save for Later button.
