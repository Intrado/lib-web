Shortcuts

Important: Only those features that you have been granted access to will display. For access to additional features contact your system administrator.

If enabled on your account, there will be some important features located at the top of your screen. They are:

  • Shortcuts - This dropdown menu contains links to the most commonly used features in the system such as New List, and Call Me to Record.

  • My Address Book - Clicking this button will open up your address book so that you may add, edit, or delete contacts for use in your lists.

  • My Account - This button allows you to view and edit your user name, password, and contact information.

  • Help - Clicking this will open up the online help documentation.

  • Logout - This will log you out of the system and return to the login screen.
