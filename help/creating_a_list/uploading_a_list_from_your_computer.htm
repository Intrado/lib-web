Uploading a List From Your Computer

Important: Only those features that you have been granted access to will display. For access to additional features contact your system administrator.

Another way to create a list is to upload a pre-existing list from your computer if this feature has been enabled on your account. Uploading a list from your computer is simple and very similar to creating a list using any of the other methods.

Note: Your list must be formatted as a Comma Separated Value (.csv) file with the fields in the following order: First Name, Last Name, Phone number with area code, email address (optional). Spreadsheet programs allow you to save your spreadsheet as a CSV file.

For example:

John,Smith,(123)456-7890,jsmith@school.edu

To Upload a List From Your Computer

  1. Click on the Notifications tab followed by the Lists tab.

  2. Click the Create New List button towards the top of the screen.

  3. Enter a name and description for your new list.

  4. Click on the Upload button, shown below, towards the bottom of the List Editor to be taken to the Upload List screen.

  1. Select the type of upload that describes your list. There are two options:

  • Contact Data: Your list contains the complete information for recipients in the following order: First Name, Last Name, Phone Number with Area Code, and Email Address (optional). From the dropdown menus, select which field the phone and email information should be mapped to. For example, if all of the phone numbers should go in the Phone 1 field, select Phone 1 from the dropdown menu.

  • ID# Lookup: Your list only contains the ID numbers for your recipients as they exist in your database. The system will look for the recipient's information based on this number.

  1. Click the Browse button to locate the file on your computer that you wish to upload.

  2. From the dropdown menus, select which field you would like the contact's phone number and email to be mapped to.

  3. Click the Preview button to preview your list or the Cancel button to exit this screen without uploading your list.

  1. If your list looks correct, click the Save button. If you need to upload a different file, click the Select Different File button. If you would like to leave without saving your list, click the Cancel button.
