Removing Individuals From Your List

You may choose to exclude individuals from any list, even though they match the rules used to create the list. There are three ways to remove individuals from your list.  

 
Removing Individuals Using the Preview Screen:

  1. Click on the Preview button on the List Editor screen.

  2. Find the individuals you wish to remove and uncheck the check boxes next to their names.

  3. Click the Done button at the top or bottom of the screen to return to the List Editor screen.

  4. View your skipped names in the Skip section of the List Editor screen. Individuals can be returned to the list by checking the box next to their names.

 
Removing Individuals Using the List Search Screen:
 

 

  1. Click on the Search button on the List Editor screen.

  2. Enter the appropriate search criteria in the rules fields.

  3. Click the Done button at the top or bottom of the screen to return to the List Editor screen.

  4. View your skipped names in the Skip section of the List Editor screen. Individuals can be returned to the list by checking the box next to their names.

 
Removing Manually Entered Individuals:
  1. Simply uncheck the check boxes next to their names in the List Editor screen.
