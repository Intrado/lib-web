Editing or Deleting an Existing List

To Edit an Existing List

  1. Click the Notifications Tab followed by the Lists subtab to enter the List Builder screen.

  2. Click the Edit link to the right of the list you wish to edit.

  3. You will be taken to the List Editor screen where you can configure your list.

 

To Delete a List

  1. Click the Notifications Tab followed by the Lists subtab to enter the List Builder screen.

  2. Click the Delete link to the right of the list you wish to delete.

Note: Deleting cannot be undone.
