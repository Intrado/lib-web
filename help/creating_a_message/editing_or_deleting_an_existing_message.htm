Editing or Deleting an Existing Message

To Edit an Existing Message

  1. Click the Notifications Tab followed by the Messages subtab to enter the Message & Survey Template Builder screen.

  2. Click the Edit link to the right of the Message you wish to edit.

  3. Depending on the message type, you will be taken to either the Phone Message Editor or Email Message Editor screen.

To Delete a Message

  1. Click the Notifications Tab followed by the Messages subtab to enter the Message & Survey Template Builder screen.

  2. Click the Delete link to the right of the message you wish to delete.

Note: Deleting cannot be undone.
