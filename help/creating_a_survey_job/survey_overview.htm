Survey Overview

Important: Only those features that you have been granted access to will display. For access to additional features contact your system administrator.

Surveys can be a great way to gather information from parents, staff, afterschool groups, and more.  

In brief, there are five basic steps to creating a survey:

  1. Assemble your messages and questions using the Creating a Survey printable PDF worksheet and guide. Also, read the help topic, Important Tips for Creating Surveys.

  2. Record your messages and questions just like you would for a regular notification job.

  3. Build a Survey Template.

  4. Test the survey by sending it to yourself.

  5. Create a List of recipients for the survey.

  6. Schedule the Survey Job.
