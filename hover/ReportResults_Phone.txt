People: The number of people targeted to receive phone notifications included in this report

Phone # Duplicates Removed: The number of duplicate phone numbers removed from this list

% Completed: The percent of the total calls that have been completed thus far

Delivered: The number of successful deliveries completed thus far

Undelivered: The number of unsuccessful deliveries at the completion of the job due to no phone number, busy signals, no answers, disconnected numbers, and any other failed delivery attempts

Remaining: The number of people targeted to receive phone notifications who have not been contacted thus far.

Success Rate: The percentage of attempted calls that were successfully delivered

Total Call Attempts: The total number of call attempts included in this report
