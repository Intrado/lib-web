People: The number of unique individuals in this report targeted to receive phone notifications

Phone # Duplicates Removed: The number of duplicate phone numbers removed from this list

% Completed: The percent of individuals who have been contacted or are no longer eligible to be attempted

Delivered: The number of successful deliveries completed thus far

Undelivered: The number of unsuccessful deliveries thus far due to no phone number, busy signals, no answers, disconnected numbers, and any other failed delivery attempts

Remaining: The number of people targeted to receive phone notifications who have not been contacted thus far
